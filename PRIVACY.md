## 中文

Mo 尊重并保护您的隐私。本应用所有数据均存储在您的本地设备上，我们不会收集、存储或传输您的任何个人信息。

### 数据存储

所有待办事项和备忘录数据均保存在应用本地数据库中，不会上传到任何服务器。

### 权限使用

- **通知权限**：仅用于待办提醒功能
- **精确闹钟**：仅用于定时提醒调度
- **开机启动**：仅用于重启后恢复提醒

### 第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

### 数据导出

您可以随时通过导出功能备份数据，数据完全由您掌控。

### 联系方式

如有隐私相关问题，请通过 GitHub 联系我们：
https://github.com/gascs/Mo-Todo

## English

Mo respects and protects your privacy. All data in this app is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.

### Data Storage

All todo items and memos are saved in the app's local database and are never uploaded to any server.

### Permissions

- **Notifications**: Used only for todo reminders
- **Exact alarms**: Used only for scheduled reminders
- **Boot receiver**: Used only to restore reminders after reboot

### Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

### Data Export

You can back up your data at any time through the export feature. Your data is fully under your control.

### Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us via GitHub:
https://github.com/gascs/Mo-Todo
